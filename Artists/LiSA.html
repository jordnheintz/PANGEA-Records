Gurenge

"Gurenge" (紅蓮華, Red Lotus) is a song by Japanese pop singer LiSA. It was released as her fifteenth single digitally on April 22, 2019, and received a physical release on July 3, 2019. It reached number 3 on Oricon, number 2 on Japan Hot 100 and number 1 on Japan Hot Animation. It was used as the opening theme song for the anime series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.

Crossing Field

Crossing field is the first opening song for the Sword Art Online anime. The single, upon its release, reached 5th place in Oricon singles weekly chart, 10th place in Billboard JAPAN Hot 100 chart, 4th place in Billboard JAPAN Hot Singles Sales chart, and 2nd place in Billboard JAPAN Hot Animation chart, all of which were on the first week of August 2012. As of February 2017, the downloads of the single had reached 500,000 hits worldwide.
