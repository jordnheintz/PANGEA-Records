Gurenge

"Gurenge" (紅蓮華, Red Lotus) is a song by Japanese pop singer LiSA. It was released as her fifteenth single digitally on April 22, 2019, and received a physical release on July 3, 2019. It reached number 3 on Oricon, number 2 on Japan Hot 100 and number 1 on Japan Hot Animation. It was used as the opening theme song for the anime series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.
