Aimer

Kataomoi

Daydream is the fourth studio album released by Aimer. "Kataomoi" (カタオモイ; "Unrequited Love") was the 7th song in the Daydream album. It currently has 20+ millions of views on Youtube.

Brave Shine

"Brave Shine" is the eighth single by Japanese singer Aimer, released on June 3, 2015, under Defstar Records. Written by Aimer (under her pen name "aimerrhythm") and Hisashi Koyama, the song was used as the second opening theme of the anime TV series Fate/stay night: Unlimited Blade Works. Initially, the series' second opening theme was intended to be "Last Stardust," also composed by Aimer. However, the staff did not find it fitting for the video's sequences and instead used "Brave Shine."
