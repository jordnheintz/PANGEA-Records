Монеточка

Monetochka (also Liza Monetochka, real name - Elizaveta Andreevna Gyrdimova; born June 1, 1998, Yekaterinburg, Russia) is a Russian singer, musician, composer and songwriter. The genres of pop-rock and antifolk prevail in Monetochka's work, and since the end of 2016 her songs have begun to contain elements of electronic music. She mainly publishes her music through the Internet and a local tv channel in Yekaterinburg.

Accomplishments:

  • Music video with over 9 million views
  • Album entered top 100 music chart 3 days after release
  • Group of the Year award (2018)
  • Single of the Year award (2018)
  • “This is absolutely a child telling you about what is happening around you, with such irreconcilability which you would never have dared to use yourself.”
  • “She managed to break the framework of the independent scene and break into the mainstream.”
We admire Монеточка because she has managed to make a name for herself and was able to make a career out of what she loves.
